Frequently Asked Questions

I lost my progress — what can I do?

Progress is stored locally on your device. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, the previous progress will not transfer automatically. We are working on cloud save support for a future update.

An ad froze or didn't reward me

If a rewarded ad failed to grant a reward, please force-close the game and reopen it — your reward will usually be granted on the next launch. If the issue persists, contact support with your device model and Android version.

How do I remove ads?

An ad-free option will be available via in-app purchase in an upcoming update.

The game crashes on launch

Please make sure you are running the latest version from Google Play, and that your device meets the minimum requirements (Android 7.0+). Reinstalling the app usually resolves crash issues.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email us at the address below — please include your device model, Android version, and a short description of what happened.
